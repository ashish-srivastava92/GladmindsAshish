Enrolment Conditions of Power Rewards

  • This scheme is a genuine parts fitment promotional scheme, to encourage and reward garage owners who recommend and use BAJAJ GENUINE PARTS.
  • Only Garage owners are eligible for participating in this program.Only one person from the participating garage is allowed to get registered in this program.
  • The mandatory clauses for enrollment the person are :- Photo identity of the person getting registered, Name, mobile number, garage name, complete address of the garage, PIN code, District/City/taluka, Clear Land Mark.
  • The enrolment onto the club for garage would be physically verified periodically during the scheme period and any discrepancies if noticed,the registration will be deactivated from the scheme. Bajaj Auto's decision will be final in such decisions.The reward points if any accumulated will stand cancelled.
  • Each Registered Garage would be physically verified periodically during the scheme period and any discrepancies if noticed, the registration will be deactivated from the scheme. Bajaj Auto's decision will be final in such decisions. The reward points if any accumulated will stand cancelled.
  • Bajaj Auto do not provide any certification for the participants for the scheme.
  • Only the CV parts having "UNIQUE CODE" sold by Bajaj CV Distributors are covered under this program.
  • The selected parts would carry "UNIQUE CODE" on its package. The reward points ar awarded when the "UNIQUE CODEs" of these parts are SMSed through the registered mobile no of the participating Garage owner.
  • Registered garage owners can accumulate such reward points during period of the scheme and can redeem them for eligible gifts.The eligible gifts with corresponding no of reward points required are listed in the scheme gift catalogue.
  • Any request for change of gifts/models other than mentioned in the gift catalogues will not be entertained. In case of any model/gift getting obsolete, BAJAJ reserves the right to offer another gift og equivalent points.
  • The gifts/alternate gifts are being provided from reputed suppliers. Bajaj Auto Ltd., and their Associates are in no way shall stand guarantee to the quality/purity of gifts nor be liable for any deficiencies, defects, warranties.
  • All applicable taxes including but not limited to income(With holding)tax, TDS, Gift tax &insurance charges or any other incidental charges for the program gifts will be payable by the respective Registered garage owner only.
  • "UNIQUE CODEs"after gaining reward points through SMS and confirmation are to be destroyed immediately to avoid duplicity/misuse.
  • Reward Points accumulated cannot be exchanged for cash.

Invalid Unique Part Code (UPC) You might receive this message if the unique part code you sms is not a valid number, please check the UPC MRP sticker again and ensure you have entered it correctly.


Used Number This message might be sent to you in case this number has already been used once in our system. It might be that you have sent the sms twice. If you have not SMSed twice and yet you get this message please send used UPC MRP sticker along with your details ( xerox of your card ) to bajaj auto limited address mentioned on the reverse of the booklet.


SMS Cannot Be Delivered If your SMS fail to reach us, you can call us on our helpline number +917847011011 to resolve the problem.


Change Of Your Phone Number If you change your mobile or address, you will have to fill in a form and send it to us. We will link your new phone number to your name and membership number.


Invalid Keyword If you received the reply SMS "Invalid Keyword". Then please check the SMS format and resend.
